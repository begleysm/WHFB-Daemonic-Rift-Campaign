Victory conditions

Assassinate

  • Massacre – All enemy characters are slain and all of your characters survived and are not fleeing
  • Major Victory -  All enemy characters are slain or fleeing and your general survived and is not fleeing
  • Minor Victory – More enemy characters killed than friendly characters
  • Draw – Any other result

Take and Hold

  • Massacre – One non-fleeing unit of unit strength ten or greater must cover the exact center point of the board and the controlling unit’s side must have achieved a standard victory point massacre
  • Major Victory – One non-fleeing unit of unit strength five or greater must cover the exact center point of the board
  • Minor Victory – One non-fleeing unit of less than unit strength five must cover the exact center point of the board
  • Draw – Neither player controls the exact center point of the board

Pitched Battle

  • Massacre- Standard victory point rules
  • Major Victory – Standard victory point rules
  • Minor Victory – Standard victory point rules
  • Draw – Standard victory point rules.

Dominion

  • Massacre – Control all four quadrants of the board per the standard rules
  • Major Victory – Control three quadrants of the board per the standard rules
  • Minor Victory – Control two quadrants of the board and your opponent only controls one per the standard rules
  • Draw – Any other result
